Typical Camp Day

  • 8 – 9 am Early care / free play (optional)
  • 9 am Morning meeting & planning
  • 9:30 – 11:45 am Morning workshops, projects, free play
  • 12 pm Lunch
  • 12:30 – 1:30 pm Afternoon meeting & planning
  • 1 – 2:45 pm Afternoon workshops, projects, free play
  • 3 pm Pick-up
  • 3:15 – 5:30 pm Late care / games / crafts (optional)

Campers choose from staff-led activities—open shop, electronics, cardboard crafts, art-making, computer programming, science projects, outdoor games, and more. They can also explore the space, work on their own projects, or play with friends. All activities are optional and we always have staff available to help campers with their own ideas.

All camp activities are optional and campers are encouraged to explore their own interests and design their own camp-day. With 8 to 12 campers per week, and 2 - 3 staff members and workshop leaders we can provide a lot of individual attention and support.
