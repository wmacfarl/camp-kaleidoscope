Registration is Open!

Join us for two weeks of arts, crafts, and creativity in Providence, RI. Camp runs from 9:00am to 3:00pm, Monday through Friday. We are also offering optional early and late care (starting at 8:00am and ending at 5:30pm) for families who need it.

  • Week 1: July 7–11
  • Week 2: July 14–18

Each week is offered separately, and you can register for one or both weeks. Cost is $350 per week, with a sliding scale and free slots available for families who need them. We are committed to making camp accessible to all families, regardless of income. For more information on our sliding scale, please see our sliding scale page.
