Registration is now open!

Join us for two weeks of arts, crafts, and creativity in Providence, RI.

  • Week 1: July 7–11
  • Week 2: July 14–18

Each week is offered separately, and you can register for one or both weeks. Cost is $350 per week, with a sliding scale and free slots available for families who need them. We are committed to making camp accessible to all families, regardless of income.

Camp Kaleidoscope is a queer-friendly two-week community summer program for kids ages 6–12. We combine play, arts, making, and self-directed learning to spark creativity and confidence.

We are proud to be partnering with Public Not Private to offer our program in at their amazing shop, studio, and gallery space at 50 Agnes Street #101, Providence, RI 02909.
