Welcome to Camp Kaleidoscope!

Registration is now open for our very first summer program in Providence! Join us for two weeks of arts, crafts, and creativity in Providence, RI.

  • Week 1: July 7–11
  • Week 2: July 14–18

Each week is offered separately, and you can register for one or both weeks.

Camp Kaleidoscope is a queer-friendly two-week community summer program for kids ages 6–12. We combine play, arts, making, and self-directed learning to spark creativity and confidence.

We are proud to be partnering with Public Not Private to offer our program in at their amazing shop, studio, and gallery space at 50 Agnes Street #101, Providence, RI 02909.
