Our Program

Camp Kaleidoscope is a two-week pop-up makerspace arts program for kids ages 6–12. We combine play, arts, making, and self-directed learning to spark creativity and confidence.

Our Schedule

9:00am–3:00pm Monday–Friday, plus optional early/late care. Activities include hands-on projects, free-build time, outdoor play, and creative group endeavors.

Our Philosophy

We believe kids thrive when given the tools, ideas, independence, and support to invent and create on their own terms.

Activities

  • Art and crafts
  • STEM explorations
  • Collaborative group projects
  • Games and free play
