Our Program

Camp Kaleidoscope is a two-week summer camp for kids ages 6–12. We combine play, arts, making, and self-directed learning to spark creativity and confidence.

Our Schedule

9:00am–3:00pm Monday–Friday, plus optional early/late care. Activities include hands-on projects, free-build time, outdoor play, and creative group endeavors.

  • Week 1: July 7–11
  • Week 2: July 14–18

The Camp Day

`

We split the day into a morning and afternoon activity period. At the beginning of the day, and after lunch, we gather to hold a brief meeting, where campers and staff can check in with each other, share ideas, be together, and plan the day. Each day staff come prepared with activities and projects that they are excited about sharing but we also leave space for campers to share their own ideas and interests. After meeting kids are free to join projects, organize their own activities, or just hang out and play.

At any given moment, campers might be playing board games, making puppets, programming computers, playing with bubbles, or running around outside. Some kids will spend all day or week working on a single project while others will move from one activity to another.

Our Philosophy

We believe kids thrive when given the tools, ideas, independence, and support to invent and create on their own terms.
