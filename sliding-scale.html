Sliding Scale

Camp Kaleidoscope is committed to making our program accessible to all families, regardless of income. We are offering a sliding scale for registration fees, with a suggested fee of $350 per week.

When you register for camp you will be asked to select a fee from the sliding scale. There is no application process, you just pay what you can. We encourage families to pay what makes sense for them for each week of camp.

We understand that all families have different financial situations, and that some families may be able to pay more or less than the suggested fee. We encourage families to pay what makes sense for them for each week of camp.

If you are unable to pay the suggested fee, that is okay! We are happy to offer free and reduced cost slots to families who need them. On the contrary, if you are able to pay more than the suggested fee, additional funds will help us offer free and reduced cost slots to families who need them and fund ongoing programming.
